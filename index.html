About Us

We are 2 middle schoolers named Evan and Dylan. This is our community project on the UN sustainable development goal of Climate Action. We hope to influence and inform people that climate change is a threat to the society of the near future so people can make climate change friendly decisions. If climate change is aborted then the next generation won't have to deal with such problems.
